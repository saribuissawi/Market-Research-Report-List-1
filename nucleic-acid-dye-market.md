Ethidium Bromide is widely used for visualizing DNA and RNA in gel electrophoresis due to its ability to intercalate between base pairs and fluoresce under UV light. Other dyes encompass a range of alternatives offering unique features for different nucleic acid detection and visualization needs in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
