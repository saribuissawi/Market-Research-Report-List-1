<p><ul><li>Protein-based Fat Replacers</li><li>Carbohydrate-based Fat Replacers</li><li>Fat-based Fat Replacers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edible fat replacers market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Protein-based fat replacers** utilize proteins, such as whey or soy, to mimic the creamy texture of fats while providing nutritional benefits. </p><p>2. **Carbohydrate-based fat replacers** rely on polysaccharides like starches or fibers to simulate thickness and mouthfeel without adding calories. </p><p>3. **Fat-based fat replacers**, often derived from modified vegetable oils, aim to replicate the flavor and texture of fats while reducing overall fat content in food products.</p></p>
