<p><strong>The Industrial Encapsulation Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-Cured</li><li>Light-Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial encapsulation adhesives market is primarily segmented into heat-cured and light-cured types. Heat-cured adhesives require elevated temperatures for polymerization, providing strong bonds suitable for high-stress applications. They are commonly used in electronics and automotive sectors. In contrast, light-cured adhesives utilize ultraviolet or visible light to initiate the curing process, allowing for quicker set times and precise application. These are ideal for scenarios requiring immediate handling, such as in medical devices and optical components, enhancing production efficiency and flexibility.</p></p>
