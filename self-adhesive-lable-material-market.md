<p><p>The self-adhesive label material market is primarily divided into two types: film substrates and paper substrates. Film substrates, often made from materials like polypropylene or polyester, offer durability, resistance to moisture, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for challenging environments. In contrast, paper substrates provide a classic look, high print quality, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications, including retail and packaging. Both types cater to specific needs, influencing their use across diverse industries.</p></p>
