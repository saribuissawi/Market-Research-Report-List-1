<p><p>Endoscope disinfectors are essential for ensuring the safety and hygiene of medical instruments. The market is primarily divided into two types: single chamber and multi-chamber disinfectors. Single chamber disinfectors offer a compact solution where endoscopes are cleaned and disinfected in one enclosed space, ideal for smaller facilities. In contrast, multi-chamber disinfectors provide separate chambers for cleaning, rinsing, and drying, allowing for more thorough processes and higher throughput, making them suitable for larger healthcare facilities with higher demands.</p></p>
