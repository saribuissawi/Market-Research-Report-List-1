<p><p>The Optical Fiber Density (FoD) chip market is categorized into two main types: "Small Hole Imaging" and "Collimated Light Path". Small Hole Imaging utilizes tiny apertures to capture high-resolution images of fiber surface defects, enhancing inspection precision in telecommunications. In contrast, the Collimated Light Path method employs parallel light beams for consistent and accurate measurements over longer distances, making it ideal for analyzing fiber characteristics. Both types contribute significantly to the advancement of optical technologies in various applications.</p></p>
