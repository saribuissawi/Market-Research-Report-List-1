<p><strong>The Gas and Oil Pipeline Leak Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass-Volume Balance Method</li><li>Acoustic/Ultrasonic</li><li>Fiber Optics</li><li>Vapor Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas and oil pipeline leak detector market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Mass-Volume Balance Method** measures discrepancies between input and output volumes to identify leaks.  </p><p>2. **Acoustic/Ultrasonic** detectors utilize sound waves to detect changes in frequency or noise caused by leaks.  </p><p>3. **Fiber Optics** systems employ light transmission through fiber cables, where any disruption indicates a potential leak.  </p><p>4. **Vapor Sensing** technologies detect the presence of specific gas or vapor compounds in the environment surrounding pipelines, signaling leaks. Each method offers distinct advantages for leak detection and management.</p></p>
