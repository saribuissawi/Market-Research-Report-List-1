<p><p>Heated rearview mirrors are designed to improve visibility in adverse weather conditions by preventing fogging and ice buildup. The market primarily comprises two types: electric heating and water pump heating. Electric heating utilizes embedded heating elements that warm the mirror surface directly, offering quick defrosting. In contrast, water pump heating circulates heated fluid to the mirror, providing a more gradual warmth. Both systems enhance safety and comfort for drivers, catering to various vehicle needs and climatic conditions.</p></p>
