<p><p>The Oral Biological Barrier Membrane market consists of two primary types: absorbable membranes and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are designed to break down naturally in the body over time, supporting tissue regeneration while eliminating the need for surgical removal. In contrast, non-absorbable membranes provide long-lasting support but require surgical intervention for removal. Both types are crucial in dental and oral surgical procedures, offering solutions for guided tissue regeneration and enhancing healing outcomes in various dental treatments.</p></p>
