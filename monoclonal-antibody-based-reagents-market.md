<p><p>The monoclonal antibody-based reagents market encompasses various assay types for detecting specific antigens. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) utilize enzyme-linked antibodies to quantify target proteins. Dot-Immunogold Filtration Assays offer rapid results using antibody-coated gold particles. The Double Antigen Sandwich Chemiluminescence Method enhances sensitivity by employing two antibodies to capture and detect the same antigen. Recombinant Immunoblot Assays combine traditional immunoblotting with recombinant antibodies for precise antigen identification. Each method has unique advantages for diagnostic applications across different industries.</p></p>
