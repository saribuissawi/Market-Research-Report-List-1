<p><p>The Continuous Subcutaneous Insulin Infusion Pump market includes three primary types: Traditional pumps, Patch pumps, and Smart pumps. Traditional pumps are standard devices delivering insulin through a catheter attached to the skin. Patch pumps are compact units that adhere directly to the skin, eliminating external tubing for ease of use. Smart pumps integrate advanced technology, offering features like real-time data monitoring, automated insulin delivery adjustments, and connectivity to mobile apps, enhancing management of diabetes care and patient engagement.</p></p>
