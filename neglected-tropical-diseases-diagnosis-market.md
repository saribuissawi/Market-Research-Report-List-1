<p><p>The Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs) diagnosis market encompasses two main types: conventional methods and molecular/modern methods. Conventional methods include traditional diagnostic techniques like microscopy, serological tests, and culture methods, which often require skilled personnel and time. In contrast, molecular/modern methods utilize advanced technologies such as PCR, next-generation sequencing, and rapid diagnostic tests, offering higher sensitivity and specificity. These modern methods enable faster results and can be employed in field settings, significantly improving the management and control of NTDs.</p></p>
