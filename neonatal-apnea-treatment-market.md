<p><p>The Neonatal Apnea Treatment Market includes several key pharmaceuticals. Caffeine citrate injection is the primary treatment, acting as a central nervous system stimulant to enhance respiratory drive. Doxapram is another option, promoting breathing by stimulating carotid chemoreceptors. Theophylline, a methylxanthine, relaxes airway muscles and improves diaphragm function. Aminophylline, a combination of theophylline and ethylenediamine, aids in respiratory support as well. Each of these treatments plays a role in managing apnea in premature infants, improving breathing stability and outcomes.</p></p>
