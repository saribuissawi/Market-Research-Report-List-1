<p><strong>The MRI Flowmeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Pulse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI flowmeter market is segmented into two primary types: continuous and pulse flowmeters. Continuous flowmeters provide a steady stream of data regarding fluid movement, ensuring real-time monitoring and accurate measurement, which is essential in various medical applications. In contrast, pulse flowmeters quantify fluid flow in discrete intervals or bursts, making them suitable for scenarios where flow dynamics change rapidly. Both types cater to specific needs within healthcare, enhancing the effectiveness of diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in MRI environments.</p></p>
