<p><p>The VR glasses market comprises three main types: external, integrated, and mobile. Integrated VR glasses feature built-in processing capabilities, allowing for standalone use without external devices, ideal for casual users. Mobile VR glasses use smartphones as their display, providing an accessible and affordable introduction to virtual reality, though typically with lower performance than the other types. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
