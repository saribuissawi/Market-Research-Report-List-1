<p><p>The automotive glass for backlites includes several types: tempered glass, laminated glass, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and shatter resistance, commonly used in rear windows. Laminated glass consists of two glass layers with a plastic interlayer, providing enhanced safety and sound insulation, often utilized in luxury vehicles. The "others" category encompasses specialized types, such as UV-filtering or tinted glass, catering to specific aesthetic or performance needs. Each type contributes to vehicle safety, comfort, and design.</p></p>
