<p><p>The GRP piping market is primarily categorized into three types: epoxy piping, polyester piping, and vinyl ester piping. Epoxy piping offers excellent chemical resistance and strength, making it suitable for harsh environments. Polyester piping is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and versatility, commonly found in industrial applications. Vinyl ester piping combines the advantages of both epoxy and polyester, providing enhanced chemical resistance and durability, often utilized in corrosive settings. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
