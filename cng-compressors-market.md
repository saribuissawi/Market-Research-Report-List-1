<p><strong>The CNG Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Reciprocating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNG compressors market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and reciprocating. Rotary compressors utilize rotating elements to compress gas efficiently and are known for their continuous operation and compact design, making them suitable for high-volume applications. In contrast, reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress gas in a cyclic manner, offering higher pressure capabilities and flexibility for various applications. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing natural gas distribution and fueling infrastructure, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
