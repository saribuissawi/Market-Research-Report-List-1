<p><p>The Rectangular Steel Hollow Section market is primarily categorized into two types: Welded Steel Hollow Section and Seamless Steel Hollow Section. Welded sections are produced by welding together flat steel plates, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility for construction and structural applications. In contrast, Seamless Steel Hollow Sections are manufactured without any seams or welds, providing enhanced strength and integrity, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
