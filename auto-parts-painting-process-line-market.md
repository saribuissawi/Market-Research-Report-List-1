<p><strong>The Auto Parts Painting Process Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Coating Line</li><li>Liquid Coating Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto parts painting process line market can be divided into two main types: powder coating lines and liquid coating lines. Powder coating lines utilize electrostatic deposition to apply a dry powder that cures under heat, offering a durable and environmentally friendly finish. In contrast, liquid coating lines involve the application of liquid paint, which can provide a smooth, high-gloss finish but may require solvent management. Both methods serve to enhance the aesthetic and protective qualities of auto parts in manufacturing.</p></p>
