<p><p>Voltage Dependent Resistors (VDRs) are passive electronic components that exhibit resistance changes based on applied voltage, providing over-voltage protection. The VDR market primarily includes two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type and Leaded Type. SMD VDRs are compact, allowing for high-density circuit designs, while Leaded VDRs feature pins for through-hole mounting, suitable for traditional circuit boards. Both types are critical in safeguarding electronic devices from voltage spikes in various applications, including consumer electronics and industrial equipment.</p></p>
