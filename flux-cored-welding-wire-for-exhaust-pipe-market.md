<p><p>Flux cored welding wire for exhaust pipe applications is categorized primarily into Metal Inert Gas (MIG) and Metal Active Gas (MAG) welding. MIG welding utilizes an inert gas shield to protect the weld from contaminants, ensuring a clean and strong joint. In contrast, MAG welding employs active gases, which can enhance the penetration and speed of the weld. Other market types may involve specialized flux cored wires that accommodate different materials or applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
