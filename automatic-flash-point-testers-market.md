<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Flash Point Testers market is divided into two key types: Open Cup Flash Point Testers and Closed Cup Flash Point Testers. Open Cup testers determine flash points using an open sample container, exposing the liquid to the environment, making them suitable for a wider range of substances. Closed Cup testers use a sealed container, providing a more controlled environment, and are typically used for volatile liquids. These distinctions impact the safety, accuracy, and regulatory compliance of testing processes.</p></p>
