<p><strong>The Photosensitive Polyimide Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photosensitive Polyimide</li><li>Negative Photosensitive Polyimide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photosensitive Polyimide Coatings market is categorized into two main types: positive photosensitive polyimides and negative photosensitive polyimides. Positive photosensitive polyimide coatings become soluble when exposed to specific wavelengths of light, allowing for easier pattern formation in microelectronics and semiconductor applications. Conversely, negative photosensitive polyimides harden upon exposure to light, remaining insoluble in developer solutions, which is beneficial for producing durable and high-resolution patterns. Both types are essential in various industries, including electronics, aerospace, and automotive, for their thermal stability and excellent insulating properties.</p></p>
