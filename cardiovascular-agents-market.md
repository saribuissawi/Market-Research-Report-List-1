<p><p>The cardiovascular agents market encompasses a variety of drug classes targeting heart and blood vessel health. Antianginal agents alleviate chest pain by improving blood flow. Antiarrhythmic agents help regulate heart rhythms. Inotropic agents enhance heart contractility. Miscellaneous agents cover diverse functions, while peripheral vasodilators widen blood vessels to improve circulation. Renin inhibitors lower blood pressure by affecting kidney function. Sclerosing agents treat vascular conditions by inducing scarring. Vasodilators relax blood vessels, promoting better circulation and reducing hypertension.</p></p>
