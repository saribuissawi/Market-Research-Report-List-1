<p><p>The Digital Baseband Unit (BBU) market serves both integrated and distributed base station applications in mobile networks. Integrated base stations combine radio and baseband processing in a single unit, enhancing efficiency and reducing infrastructure costs. In contrast, distributed base stations separate the BBU from radio units, allowing for optimized signal coverage and flexibility in deployment. Both applications are critical for improving network performance, enabling faster data rates, and supporting the growing demand for mobile connectivity in urban and rural areas.</p></p>
