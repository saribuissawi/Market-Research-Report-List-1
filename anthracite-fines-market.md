<p><p>The Anthracite Fines market is divided into two primary types based on calorific value: Low Calorific Value (LCV) and High Calorific Value (HCV). LCV anthracite fines offer a lower energy output, typically used in applications where cost-efficiency is prioritized, like warm combustion processes. In contrast, HCV anthracite fines provide higher energy content, making them suitable for industries requiring efficient fuel sources, such as power generation and metallurgical processes. Demand for each type is influenced by energy costs and environmental regulations.</p></p>
