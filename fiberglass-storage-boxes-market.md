<p><p>Fiberglass storage boxes are categorized based on the type of glass used in their construction. E-glass (Electrical glass) is known for its high strength-to-weight ratio and electrical insulation properties, making it suitable for a variety of applications. C-glass (Chemical glass) is resistant to corrosion and ideal for chemical storage. A-glass (Alkali glass) offers superior mechanical properties and durability. Other types may include combinations or specialized glasses tailored for specific industries, enhancing performance and storage versatility.</p></p>
